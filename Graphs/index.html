Graph Making Program

Instructions:

1- Draw whatever you want in the black canvas
2- Press F12 and go to the console tab
3- Copy the text (if it's big enough a "copy" button will appear)
4- Open desmos graphing calculator
5- Paste the copied text in the tab in the left
6- Click to add a slider z
7- Zoom out until you see x and y 500
8- Ajust z to improve the image (has to be positive, try 100 to start)
