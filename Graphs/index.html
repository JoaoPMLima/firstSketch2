Graph Making Program

Instructions:

1- Draw anything you want in the black canvas

2- Press F12

3- Copy the text (if it's big enough a "copy" button will appear)

4- Open desmos graphing calculator

5- Paste the copied text

6- Click to add a slider z

7- Zoom out to x(0 to 500) and y(0 to 500)

8- Ajust z to improve it
